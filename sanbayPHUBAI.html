Review

Quang Chính @Chinhle

The Danang International Airport is new. Quite a huge airport with nice marble floor. Check-in counters are in a row as we are early to the airport, the checking in queue wasn't long. Two toilets at both ends.There are a number of immigration counters with one specific for ASEAN and Diplomats. Security checks are strict as expected. Several Duty Free shops have opened and boarding gates (1 -10) are located at the floor below. There is a Burger King outlet and a cafe for some snacks or fast food. The departure lounge is still not fully occupied and renovation work for more outlets in progress. Airport free wifi is easily accessible...Bon voyage.

On the way, he gave us some useful information about popular destinations in Da Nang and Hoi An. We had a safe and memorable trip. Highly recommended!
